
# Privacy Policy for InnerSnap

**Effective Date:** May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the **InnerSnap** mobile application ("the App"). The App is developed and maintained by an independent developer ("I", "me", or "my"). 

My core philosophy is privacy by design. **InnerSnap operates entirely offline.** It does not require an internet connection to function, nor does it collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**InnerSnap does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any of your personal information.** All data generated by the App remains strictly on your local device. Because I do not collect any data, I do not share data with any third-party analytics providers, advertising networks, or other external services.

## 2. Device Permissions

To provide its core functionality, InnerSnap requires access to specific features on your device. These permissions are used exclusively on your device and are never used to track you or gather data for external use.

* **Camera:** Required to allow you to take pictures within the App.
* **Storage (Photos/Media/Files):** Required to save the pictures you take to your device's local storage and to access them within the App.
* **Location:** Required to tag your photos with geographical location data (e.g., saving location metadata into the image file). Your location is calculated locally on your device and is never transmitted to me or any third party.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's settings, though doing so may limit the App's functionality.

## 3. Third-Party Links and Monetization

InnerSnap is a fully featured, free application with no advertisements. 

The App contains a "Buy me a coffee" support link. If you choose to support the development of this App, clicking the link will redirect you to an external, third-party website via your device's web browser. InnerSnap does not process payments, nor does it have access to your financial or payment information. Please review the privacy policy of the third-party platform for information on how they handle your data.

## 4. Your Rights under GDPR and CalOPPA

InnerSnap is designed to be fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and Switzerland, as well as the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Under these regulations, you have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, rectify, or erase your information. 

**Because InnerSnap does not collect or hold any of your data, there is no data stored on external servers to access, update, or delete.** You have complete control over your data directly on your device. To "delete" your data, you simply need to delete the photos stored on your device or uninstall the App.

## 5. Children's Privacy

InnerSnap does not address anyone under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since the App operates completely offline and collects no data, it poses no risk to children's privacy.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App does not connect to the internet, you will not receive push notifications regarding policy updates. Any changes will be reflected in the App store description and the hosted version of this document. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 7. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy shall be governed and construed in accordance with the laws of Switzerland, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.
